Quiz Categories


An explanantion of each game category to help you choose what best suits your interest!

Animated monsters

If you're looking for somethning a little less scary, this is the category for you! This category will cover monsters from animated movies over the years. So if Disney and Dreamworks is more your thing, you'll certainly do well in this game.

Classic monsters

These beasts were the foundation of monster movies, and made history with their revolutionary effects and iconic characters, still revered to this day. If the black and white era is your expertise, give this category a go!

Horror monsters

This is the category for those who like their movies to be scary. Blood and guts galore, you're in for a scare if you choose these questions!
